Machine Learning & The Human Experience

Can ML Capture the Human Psyche?

Opponents and proponents alike have similar understandings on the capabilities of artificial intelligence and it's ability to surpass humans in logic-based activities like Go, Chess, and other games. However, some fear that artificial intelligence and it's increased usage is leading us towards a path of destruction; that is to say, humans will be completley overtaken by these technical creations of man in the next 120 years. However, I subscribe to the ideology that it is impossible for artificial intelligence and machine learning to replicate the one thing that makes every human on Earth unique: the human experience.
